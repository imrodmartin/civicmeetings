FAQ

Common Questions

Does this work with our existing website?

Yes. CivicMeetings.us is a hosted system that works independently of your website. Your meeting records live at yourtown.civicmeetings.us and can be linked from or embedded into any existing site — regardless of what CMS it runs.

Do we need a developer to set this up?

No. The system is designed for clerks and administrative staff, not developers. If you can upload a file and fill out a form, you can run CivicMeetings.us. The Guided Launch tier includes a live setup session if you want hands-on help.

Can AI automatically make our PDFs compliant?

Not reliably — and we won't pretend otherwise. Genuine PDF accessibility requires correct document structure from the start. Our document templates solve this at the source so every PDF your staff creates is compliant before it's ever uploaded.

What happens to our data if we cancel?

Your meeting records are retained for 90 days after cancellation so you can export everything. We never delete records immediately — public meeting records are important documents and we treat them that way.

Does this cover all our ADA obligations?

CivicMeetings.us addresses the public meeting records component of your ADA digital accessibility obligations. Your website as a whole has additional requirements. We focus on doing this one thing exceptionally well.

What if regulations change after we sign up?

Platform updates roll out automatically to all clients as DOJ guidance and WCAG standards evolve. The Maintenance Plan specifically includes compliance monitoring and proactive updates when the landscape shifts.
